You can save screen locations, both zoom level and center position using 10 available save slots. Press "+" in the the desired slot to save map. Use pen to rename location or trash to remove.
Using shortcuts: Press alt-key and a one digit to save the location to that number. To go to that location just press that number. (e.g. press "alt+2" to save. Then press "2" to go to that location)
