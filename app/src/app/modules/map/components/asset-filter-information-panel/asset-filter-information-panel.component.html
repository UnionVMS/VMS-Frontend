The filter field is used for filtering ships that will be shown on the map.
You can filter ships by name in this field. The filter will try to find any ship containing that letter combination.

Example

The filter query as will give you ships such as Askungen but also ships like Basto.
Advanced filters:

!

By starting with an exlamation mark the filter will instead try to find any ship not containing the given text.
Will only work on searches with type text.

Example

The filter query !as will give you all ships not shown in the previous example.

/f

By starting with /f the filter will instead filter on flagstate.
Type: text

Example

  • /f swe will give you all swedish ships.
  • /f swe,dnk will give you all swedish and danish ships.
  • /f !swe,dnk will give you all ships that are not swedish or danish.

/i

By starting with /i the filter will instead filter on ircs.
Type: text

/c

By starting with /c the filter will instead filter on cfr.
Type: text

/v

By starting with /v the filter will instead filter on vessel type.
Type: text

/e

By starting with /e the filter will instead filter on external marking.
Type: text

/l

By starting with /l the filter will instead filter on ship length.
Type: number

Example

  • /l 9.23 will give you all ships with the exact length of 9.23 meters.
  • /l 9 will give you all ships with the exact length of 9.00 meters.
  • /f ~9 will give you all ships that have a length between 9 and 10 meters, i.e. 9, 9.55, 9.99 and so forth.
  • /f >9 will give you all ships that have a length above 9 meters, i.e. 10, 19.23, 45.72 and so forth.
  • /f <9 will give you all ships that have a length below 9 meters, i.e. 8.99, 7 and so forth.
  • /f <9,>25 will give you all ships that have a length below 9 meters or above 25 meters.
  • /f <=9 will give you all ships that have a length less than or equal to 9 meters.
  • /f >=9 will give you all ships that have a length greater than or equal to 9 meters.

/m

By starting with /m will give all assets with mobileterminal on map
Type: boolean

Example

  • /m true will give you all ships with VMS
  • /m t will give you all ships with VMS

&

Is a separator, by using it you can filter on multiple things. All arguments given to the filter must be true for the ships to be visible.

Example

  • /f swe & ask will give you all swedish ships that have ask in the name.
  • ask & /f swe,dnk will give you all swedish and danish ships that have ask in the name.
  • !ask & /f swe,dnk will give you all swedish and danish ships that dosn't have ask in the name.
  • /l >10 & /l <20 will give you all ships that's bigger then 10 meters and smaller then 20 meters.
